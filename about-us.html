About Us

History of Sazee Global Enterprises

About 21 years ago, one Hajia Madinat Umar started business as a wholesaler trading in the open market (Central Market) selling various kind of consumer needs. In 2007 precisely, the relationship with P&G began with Always and Pampers brands only. In November, 2015 P&G pioneered the Strategic Distribution Model and SGE were selected among the few qualified distributors.

In June 2019, SGE was incorporated for the purpose of focusing on P&G business. Our initial area of coverage was 30% of Kaduna city, Kafanchan & Kujama cities. Today, we are one of the major distributors in Kaduna State, and we have reached other cities like Funtua, Birnin Kebbi and others.

We are also affiliated to Hayat Kimya Nigeria Limited; the world's 5th largest branded baby diapers(Molfix) manufacturer, and the largest paper tissue manufacturer as one of their major distributors in Kaduna State. We also recently acquired dealership of Kisskids baby diapers.

We market products like pampers, Oral-B, SafeGuard, Ariel, Always sanitary pads, Gillette, molfix, familia tissue, Bama, kisskids diapers and so on.
